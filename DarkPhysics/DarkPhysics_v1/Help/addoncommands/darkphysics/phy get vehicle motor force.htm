PHY GET VEHICLE MOTOR FORCE

Determine the motor force of a vehicle.

  Syntax 
float = phy get vehicle motor force ( ID )
  Parameters 

ID
identification number of the vehicle

  Returns

motor force of a vehicle

  Description

The motor force of a vehicle represents how much force is being applied to the motor. For example, if 0.0 is returned then no force is being applied whereas if 100.0 is returned then a considerable amount of force is being applied to the motor and thus the vehicle will be moving at quite some speed.
